Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all contact form submissions within 24 hours during business days.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes, we work with businesses of all sizes to create tailored inventory management solutions.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 14-day free trial for all new business customers with full platform access.

What industries do you serve?

We work with retail, e-commerce, hospitality, and any business affected by weather patterns.

Is my business data safe with WeatherWise?

Absolutely. WeatherWise implements end-to-end encryption, secure cloud storage, and role-based access control to ensure your data remains protected from unauthorized access and breaches.

How does WeatherWise use AI for inventory forecasting?

WeatherWise uses artificial intelligence to analyze real-time weather data alongside historical sales and inventory trends. This allows the system to predict demand changes, optimize stock levels, and prevent overstock or shortages before they happen.
